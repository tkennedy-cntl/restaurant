Free Cow


Inspiration

The consumption of beef is one of the major (and growing) sources of greenhouse gasses. The 1.2 Billion cows we raise to be eaten are belching us into oblivian. Our goal is to provide our customers with "Free of Cow" dining experience to support a healthier planet.

Native Farm Raised Fish
Native Farm Raised Fish Recipies

Our fish dishes come from locally farmed native fish grown in healthy sustainable farm ecosystems with the highest level of quality and safety. Our farms are nationally recognized for their taste, quality and low environmental impact. Being native, our fish are non-invasive and happy in our farms.
